# 💧 AquaPay

**A Web-Based Payroll, Ordering, and Sales Management System**

## 📌 Overview

**AquaPay** is a web-based system designed to automate and integrate **payroll management**, **ordering**, and **sales tracking** for small to medium-sized enterprises (SMEs), particularly water refilling stations.

It replaces manual and spreadsheet-based processes with a **centralized, efficient, and secure platform**, improving accuracy, productivity, and decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Automate payroll computation and salary generation
* Streamline customer order processing
* Record and monitor sales transactions in real-time
* Integrate digital payments (GCash)
* Provide centralized reporting and data management

---
