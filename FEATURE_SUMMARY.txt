   Dashboard → Products
        ↓
   "+ Add Product" button
        ↓

   Upload image (optional)
        ↓
   "Save Product" button
        ↓
   ✅ Product added to catalog


🟢 EDIT EXISTING PRODUCT
   Dashboard → Products
        ↓
   Find product in list
        ↓

        ↓

        ↓
   Upload new image (optional)
        ↓

        ↓
   ✅ Changes saved

   Dashboard → Products
        ↓
   Find product in list
        ↓
   Click "Delete" button
        ↓
   Confirm deletion
        ↓
   ✅ Product removed
   ✅ Image auto-cleaned
